Company Profile

ABOUT

Adidas, a global sports brand synonymous with performance and style, has left an indelible mark on the world of athletics and fashion. Founded in 1949 by Adi Dassler in Herzogenaurach, Germany, the company has grown into one of the most recognizable and influential sportswear brands globally. With its iconic three-stripe logo and commitment to innovation, Adidas has consistently pushed the boundaries of sportswear, setting trends and inspiring athletes and enthusiasts a like.



OUR TEAM

At Adidas, our team is the heartbeat of our brand's success. Comprised of diverse and passionate individuals from various backgrounds and expertise, we are united by our shared commitment to innovation, excellence, and a deep love for sports and style. Together, we harness our collective creativity, drive, and expertise to design, produce, and market cutting-edge athletic wear and footwear that not only empowers athletes to reach their peak performance but also resonates with fashion-conscious consumers worldwide. Our collaborative spirit and dedication to pushing boundaries enable us to stay at the forefront of the industry, ensuring that every Adidas product reflects our brand's legacy of quality, authenticity, and innovation. We take pride in our team's relentless pursuit of excellence, and it is this spirit that drives us to continue shaping the future of sportswear.



OUR MISSION

Athletes do not settle for average. And neither do we. We have a clear mission: To be the best sports brand in the world. Every day, we come to work to create and sell the best sports products in the world, and to offer the best service and consumer experience – and to do it all in a sustainable way. We are the best when we are the credible, inclusive, and sustainable leader in our industry.
